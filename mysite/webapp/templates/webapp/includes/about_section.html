New Furniture for Home Decoration

 Our furniture is constructed of solid wood by A&D Furniture builders from Holmes, Wayne and Tuscarawas counties in Ohio’s Amish country. This area boasts the largest concentration of hardwood furniture builders in the United States.
 The selection we have in the store and on the website is only a sliver of available styles. Visit the store and browse our catalogs to see other pieces. If you’re out of town, contact Wood Grains owner Dulcie Hankins, who can help you find the right furnishings for your home and have them shipped directly to you.
 We want you to have exactly the piece you imagine. Customize the size, wood species, finish or fabric on almost any furnishing in our builders’ collections. We will work to ensure the final product meets your specifications. We can also match stains and finishes of your existing furniture.
